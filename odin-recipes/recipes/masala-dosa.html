Masala Dosa

A South Indian delicacy featuring a crispy crepe filled with a spiced potato mixture and served with coconut chutney.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Salt
  • Potato Masala
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • Curry leaves
  • Mustard seeds
  • Green chilies
  • Turmeric
  • 2 cups rice

Steps:

  1. Grind into a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  2. Prepare potato masala by sautéing mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, and chilies.
  3. Add turmeric and mashed potatoes.
  4. Spread dosa batter on a hot tawa.
  5. Add oil around edges and cook until crispy.
  6. Place potato filling inside and fold.
  7. Serve with chutney and sambar.
  8. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 6 hours.
